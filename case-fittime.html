The Problem

Screen blockers don't actually work

80% of people feel guilty about their screen time but lack the willpower to stop. Existing solutions like Forest gamify avoidance, and StayFree tracks usage — but none introduce a meaningful "cost" for opening a distracting app.

The result? Blockers get bypassed in seconds. People need friction that feels rewarding — not punishment, but a trade. FitTime flips the model: the cost of distraction is physical exercise, and the reward is screen time you genuinely earned.

The Solution

A physical paywall powered by Vision AI

FitTime integrates Google ML Kit Pose Detection to count reps in real-time through the phone camera. No wearable needed. The AI watches your body as you do push-ups or sit-ups, counts each valid rep, and unlocks the blocked app once the quota is met.

The Android Accessibility Service handles robust app-blocking at the OS level — not just a timer overlay. Combined with a gamified rank system, the app creates a positive feedback loop: more reps = better rank = more rewards.

Development Process

Design → Build → Ship

Design
UX Research & Onboarding Flow
Mapped 5-step onboarding: Name → Method → Exercise → Habit Survey → Goal. Dark-themed premium aesthetic with modular component architecture. Solved small-screen scrolling with SingleChildScrollView and bottom-anchored CTAs.
Vision AI
ML Kit Pose Detection Integration
Google ML Kit Pose Detection detects 33 body landmarks per frame. Custom rep-counting algorithm analyzes elbow, shoulder, and hip angles to validate push-up and sit-up form. Optimized for 30fps on mid-range Android devices.
Native Bridge
Android Accessibility Service
Implemented a foreground Accessibility Service that monitors the currently active app window. When a blocked app is detected, the service fires an overlay with the exercise unlock screen. Handled Play Protect warnings and ensured service persistence across device restarts.
Deployment
CI/CD & App Store Prep
GitHub Actions automated Flutter builds for both Android (APK + AAB) and iOS (Xcode archive). Configured code signing, provisioning profiles, and ML pipeline optimization for Apple's Neural Engine.

Testing & Iteration

Real bugs, real solutions

Bug
UI Misalignment on small screens
Resolved by replacing fixed-size containers with Flexible and Expanded widgets, ensuring dynamic centering across all screen sizes.
Bug
Missing "Continue" button on small devices
Wrapped onboarding in SingleChildScrollView with bottom-anchored actions — button always visible regardless of content height.
Challenge
Play Protect warnings on sideloaded builds
Implemented proper APK signing and Play Protect whitelisting guidance in onboarding to reduce user drop-off during setup.
Challenge
Accessibility Service killed in background
Added persistent foreground notification, battery optimization exemption prompt, and auto-restart broadcast receiver for device reboots.
